Ingredients:
 - 250g halloumi
 - 4 tbsps flour
 - 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
 - 1 tsp flavouring (chilli/cumin)
 - 100ml water room temp
 - 1 tsp salt
 - 1/2 cup frying oil

Directions:
 1. Heat oil in saucepan to high heat.
 
 2. Mix flour, soda, flavouring and water together and whisk well until smooth.
 	Cut halloumi into thick slices and cover in mixed batter.
 
 3. Place halloumi in hot oil and turn after 3 minutes. Remove after another 2 minutes and place in kitchen towel to remove excess oil.
